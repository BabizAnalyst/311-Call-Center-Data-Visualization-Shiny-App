Los Angeles 311 call/request systems data analysis


Introduction

     This shiny app is aming to provide an easy path to the public to access the information they want to know about the Los Angeles Non-emergency 311 call/request systems. It allows people to investigate the data that they are interested in and extract meaning from the big data effectively by themselves.

Data sets

     To obtain a comprehensive insight, 311 Call Tracking Data with date range between 2011/01/01 and 2014/12/31(four years) and MyLA Services Request Data with created date ranging from 2016/01/01 to 2016/09/30 (three seasons) were selected for analysis.

The detailed and full dataset can be retrived from the following websites:

  • https://data.lacity.org/dataset/311-Call-Center-Tracking-Data/vmc3-stgb
  • https://data.lacity.org/A-Well-Run-City/MyLA311-Service-Request-Data-2016/ndkd-k878
